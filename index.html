How it works

FOUR STEPS.
ONE LOOP.

No followers required. No algorithm to game. Just your instincts, a moodboard, and a deadline. If you're right — you win things that used to be reserved for people with a PR list.

01
🎨
The Call
Build a moodboard. Attach a specific, binary prediction — something that resolves YES or NO by a deadline. No vague takes.
02
⚡
The Back
Other users back your call with Pulse Points. Fast backing = trending prediction. First 10% of backers earn a bonus if it hits.
03
✓
The Resolve
Deadline hits. The system checks the source — runway archive, retail listing, Google Trends, social count. YES or NO. Immutable.
04
🎁
The Earn
Win and redeem real rewards — brand products, early access drops, event invites, gifting packages. The influencer economy, democratized.

For Creators

THE INFLUENCER
ECONOMY,
UNLOCKED.

Verified creators on Pulse don't just post — they predict, get backed, and build a track record. And their followers can make predictions about them.

✦ Verified Predictor
Verified creators make public calls. Their accuracy score is prominent and public — a strong track record makes them a signal in itself. High accuracy unlocks brand gifting and collab opportunities without needing a PR list.
👁 Prediction Subject
Fans make binary predictions about creators — will they launch a brand, drop a collab, change their aesthetic? Creators get notified and can comment, boosting engagement and keeping their audience watching closely.
⭐ Brand Sponsored
Brands pay creators to make predictions in their category. Clearly labeled. Creator earns a fee, their community engages through backing, and the brand gets cultural data + organic content — all in one placement.
